When your brand is registered for an event, you can view the event details and your booth assignment from the exhibitor dashboard.

## What you will learn

- How to see your event list
- How to check your booth details

## Your events

From the dashboard, each card represents one brand-event. Click a card to see:

- **Event name** and dates
- **Venue** information
- **Your booth number**, type, and size
- **Fascia name** - the name on your booth signage
- **Badge name** - the name on your exhibitor badges

## Booth information

Booth details (number, type, size, location) are set by the event organizer. If anything looks wrong, contact them directly to get it corrected.

### Custom fields

Some events collect extra information from exhibitors through custom fields. If you see additional form fields on your booth profile, fill them in as requested.

## Common questions

**Q: Can I change my booth type or location?**

No. Booth assignments are managed by the event organizer. Reach out to them for changes.

**Q: Where can I see the floor plan?**

PM One does not display floor plans. Check with the event organizer for a floor plan document.
