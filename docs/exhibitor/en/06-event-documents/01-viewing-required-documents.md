Each event has rules and documents that exhibitors must acknowledge or submit before the event. Your dashboard tracks which ones are done and which need attention.

## What you will learn

- Where to find your required documents
- The difference between event rules and operational documents

## Finding your documents

1. Navigate to your brand-event
2. Go to the **Documents** section
3. You will see a list of documents required for your booth type

## Types of documents

### Event rules

These are agreements you need to accept. Read the terms and check the box to confirm. Examples: exhibition rules, safety regulations, insurance requirements.

### Operational documents

These require you to upload files or fill in information. Examples: permits, insurance certificates, floor plan submissions.

## Status indicators

Each document shows whether it is:

- **Pending** - you have not completed it yet
- **Completed** - you have agreed or submitted

Your dashboard progress bar reflects how many documents you have completed.

## Common questions

**Q: Why do some documents not apply to me?**

Documents can be set to apply only to certain booth types. You only see documents relevant to your booth.

**Q: A document I already agreed to is showing as pending again.**

The event organizer may have updated the document content. When rules change, you need to review and re-agree.
