Staying on top of your document requirements is straightforward. Your dashboard and the documents section both show your completion status at a glance.

## What you will learn

- How to check your completion status
- What to do if something is overdue

## Checking status

### From the dashboard

Your brand-event card shows a progress indicator for documents. A green checkmark means all documents are completed. Partial completion shows how many are left.

### From the documents section

The full documents list shows the status of each individual document: completed or pending.

## What to do if items are pending

1. Open the pending document
2. Follow the instructions (agree to rules or upload files)
3. Submit

If you are unsure what a document requires, contact the event organizer for clarification.

## Timeline

The event organizer may set deadlines for document completion. Check any deadline information displayed on the documents page or in communications from the organizer.

Completing documents early avoids last-minute stress and potential access restrictions.

## Common questions

**Q: What happens if I do not complete all documents?**

The event organizer decides the consequences. Incomplete documents could affect your booth access or participation. Contact them for specifics.

**Q: Can the event organizer see my submission history?**

Yes. They see who submitted each document, when, and what was uploaded.
