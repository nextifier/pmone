The order form settings control what exhibitors see when they place orders: instructions, deadlines, discount policies, and penalty rates.

## What you will learn

- How to configure order form instructions
- How to set deadlines and penalty rates

## Accessing order form settings

1. Open your event
2. Go to **Operational > Order form settings**

## Configuration options

### Instructions

Write instructions that appear at the top of the order form. Use this to explain the ordering process, payment terms, or any special requirements.

### Order period

Set the start and end dates for when exhibitors can place orders. Outside this window, the order form is locked.

### Deadlines

Define order deadlines with associated conditions. For example, orders placed after a certain date might incur a late fee.

### Penalty rates

Set penalty percentages for late orders. This rate gets applied to orders submitted after the deadline.

### Discount policies

Configure whether discounts are available, and if so, what types (fixed amount, percentage) and who can apply them.

## Common questions

**Q: Can exhibitors still view the order form after the deadline?**

They can view their existing orders, but they cannot create new ones or modify submitted orders after the order period ends.

**Q: Where do exhibitors see the instructions?**

Instructions appear at the top of the order form in the exhibitor dashboard, before the product listing.
