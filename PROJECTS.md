# PM One Projects & Events Portfolio

PM One is a comprehensive event management platform built for [Panorama Media Group](https://panoramamedia.co.id), one of Indonesia's leading exhibition and event organizers. The platform manages 11 event websites, brand/exhibitor databases, ticketing, blog content, gallery, and more through a centralized Laravel API backend consumed by individual Nuxt 4 event websites.

This document provides complete, structured information about every project and event managed through PM One, intended for both human readers and LLM consumption.

9. **Brand Talks** - Podcast-style sessions discussing products, designs, technology, and innovations from participating brands. Each brand gets a 30-minute spotlight.
10. **Ceramics Artistic Showcase** - Ceramic artists demonstrate pottery techniques, both traditional and modern, using local clay. Live demonstrations that invite visitors to try.
11. **Designer Workshops** - Collaboration with IAI and HDII for workshops, training, and educational programs for their members attending the exhibition.
12. **Building Contractors & Property Developers Members Gathering** - Joint event with REI and AKI. Networking and sharing among members during the exhibition.
13. **Seminars, Forum, Talkshows** - With media partners such as Tender Indonesia, Archinesia, and Indonesia Design. Sessions bringing insights and discussions for the ceramics exhibition target audience.
14. **Jakarta Design Week 2026** - Collaboration with IAI and DKI Jakarta Provincial Government. Forum and conference addressing architectural challenges and innovations for the city's future.
15. **Floor & Wall Tile Installation Competition** - Tile installers compete using products from Keramika exhibitors. Show your skills and win Rp5 million prize.
16. **Keramika Charity** - Exhibitors donate selected products for sale to visitors. All proceeds go directly to charitable foundations.
17. **Keramika Awards 2026** - Awards for the best in Indonesia's ceramics industry. With HDII, IAI, and GBCI, recognizing Best Design, Best Finishes, Color, and more.
18. **Keramika Best Booth** - Booth creativity appreciation in four categories: large (>108 sqm), medium (50-108 sqm), small (<50 sqm), and Visitor's Favorite.
