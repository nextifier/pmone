Your profile is what other team members see when they look you up. Settings control your password and how the app looks.

## What you will learn

- How to edit your profile info
- How to change your password
- How to switch between light and dark mode

## Editing your profile

1. Click your avatar in the top-right corner
2. Select **Profile** from the dropdown
3. Update any of these fields: display name, phone number, job title, company name, bio, and social links
4. Upload a new avatar by clicking the photo area
5. Click **Save**

Your avatar shows up next to your posts, comments, and activity entries across PM One.

## Changing your password

1. Go to **Settings > Password** from the sidebar or your avatar dropdown
2. Enter your current password
3. Enter and confirm your new password
4. Click **Update password**

If you signed up with Google and never set a password, you will see a "set password" form instead.

## Appearance

Go to **Settings > Appearance** to toggle between light mode, dark mode, or system default. The change applies right away.

## Common questions

**Q: I forgot my password and can't log in.**

Use the "Forgot password" link on the login page. You will receive a reset link by email.

**Q: Can I change my email address?**

Email changes are not self-service. Ask your admin to update it for you.
