Tickets define the admission types available for your event. Each ticket type has its own name, description, and details that appear on the event website.

## What you will learn

- How to create ticket types
- How to edit and reorder tickets

## Creating a ticket type

1. Open your event and go to **Content > Tickets**
2. Click **Create**
3. Fill in the ticket name, description, price info, and any relevant details
4. Click **Save**

Tickets show up on your event website's ticket section, giving visitors the information they need to register or purchase.

## Editing tickets

Click any ticket in the list to update its details. Changes reflect on the event website as soon as you save.

## Reordering

Drag and drop ticket types to control the order they appear on the website.

## Common questions

**Q: Does PM One handle ticket sales or payments?**

No. PM One manages ticket information (names, descriptions, links). Actual sales happen through whatever ticketing platform you link to.

**Q: Can I hide a ticket type without deleting it?**

Delete it and restore from trash when you need it again, or edit the description to indicate it is sold out.
