The gallery section lets you upload photos for your event. These appear on the event website's gallery page.

## What you will learn

- How to upload photos
- How to manage and reorder gallery images

## Uploading photos

1. Open your event and go to **Content > Gallery**
2. Click **Upload** or drag files into the upload area
3. You can upload multiple images at once

Images are automatically resized into responsive versions for fast loading on the event website.

## Managing the gallery

- **Reorder** images by dragging them into your preferred sequence
- **Delete** images by selecting them and clicking delete
- **Bulk upload** by selecting multiple files at once

## Common questions

**Q: What image formats are supported?**

JPG, PNG, and WebP. For best results, upload high-resolution photos (at least 1200px wide). The system generates smaller versions automatically.

**Q: Is there a limit on how many photos I can upload?**

There is no hard limit, but keep it practical. Large galleries take longer to load on the event website.
