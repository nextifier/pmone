Partners are the sponsors, media partners, and supporting organizations listed on your event website. You manage them per event.

## What you will learn

- How to add partners
- How to organize partners by tier

## Adding a partner

1. Open your event and go to **Content > Partners**
2. Click **Create**
3. Fill in the partner name, logo, website URL, and tier/category (e.g., "Gold Sponsor", "Media Partner")
4. Click **Save**

## Organizing partners

Partners display on the event website grouped by tier. Reorder partners within a tier by drag and drop. Reorder tiers by changing their sort order.

## Editing and removing

Click a partner to edit. Use the delete button to remove one. Deleted partners disappear from the event website immediately.

## Common questions

**Q: Can a partner appear in multiple events?**

Partners are event-specific. You will need to add them separately to each event.
