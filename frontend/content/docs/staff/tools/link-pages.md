Link pages are landing pages with a collection of links, similar to Linktree. Use them to give visitors a single URL that leads to multiple destinations: registration, social media, download pages, etc.

## What you will learn

- How to create a link page
- How to add and manage items
- How to track page analytics

## Creating a link page

1. Go to **Link Pages** in the sidebar
2. Click **Create**
3. Set a title and slug for the page
4. Click **Save**

Your link page is now accessible at `pmone.id/p/your-slug` (or whatever your domain is).

## Adding items

1. Open your link page
2. Click **Add item**
3. Enter the item title and destination URL
4. Toggle the item to active or inactive
5. Click **Save**

Items appear as buttons on the page. Inactive items are hidden from visitors.

## Reordering items

Drag and drop items to change the order they appear on the page.

## Analytics

Open the **Analytics** tab on your link page to see view counts and click-through rates for individual items.

## Deleting and restoring

Delete a link page to take it offline. Deleted pages go to the trash. Restore from **Link Pages > Trash**.

## Common questions

**Q: Can I customize the look of a link page?**

The visual style follows PM One's default design. Custom themes are not available.

**Q: Can I embed a link page on another website?**

Link pages are standalone URLs. You can link to them from anywhere, but they are not embeddable as iframes.
