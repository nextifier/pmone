The order of your promotion posts controls which ones visitors see first on the event website. Put your strongest content at the top.

## What you will learn

- How to reorder posts
- How to reorder images within a post

## Reordering posts

1. Go to your promotion posts list
2. Drag and drop posts into your preferred order
3. The new order saves automatically

The first post in the list appears first on the event website.

## Reordering images within a post

When editing a post with multiple images, drag and drop the image thumbnails to change their display sequence.

## Common questions

**Q: Does the order update on the website right away?**

Yes. The event website reflects the new order after its cache refreshes, which usually takes a few minutes.
