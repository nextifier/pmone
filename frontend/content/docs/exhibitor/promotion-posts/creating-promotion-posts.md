Promotion posts let you showcase your products, services, or brand story on the event website. Visitors browsing your brand page will see these posts with images and descriptions.

## What you will learn

- How to create a promotion post
- What the post limit means

## Creating a post

1. Navigate to your brand-event (from the dashboard or brand page)
2. Go to the **Promotion posts** section
3. Click **Create**
4. Add a caption or description
5. Upload one or more images
6. Click **Save**

Your post goes live on the event website after saving.

## Post limit

The event organizer may set a maximum number of promotion posts per brand. Check your dashboard to see how many posts you have created vs. the allowed limit.

If you have reached the limit, delete an existing post to make room for a new one.

## Writing good captions

Keep captions short and specific. Describe what is in the image, what product or service it represents, and why a visitor should care. Avoid generic marketing language.

## Common questions

**Q: Can I edit a post after publishing it?**

Yes. Click the post to edit its caption or images.

**Q: How long does it take for my post to appear on the website?**

Posts appear as soon as you save them. The event website may cache content briefly, so allow a few minutes.
