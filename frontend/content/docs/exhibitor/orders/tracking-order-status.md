After you submit an order, the event organizer processes it. You can check the status anytime from your brand-event page.

## What you will learn

- How to view your orders
- What the status labels mean

## Viewing your orders

1. Navigate to your brand-event
2. Go to the **Orders** section
3. Click any order to see its details

## Status types

Each order has two status indicators:

### Operational status

- **Pending** - order received, not yet processed
- **In Progress** - the organizer is working on it
- **Completed** - order fulfilled

### Payment status

- **Unpaid** - payment not received
- **Partially Paid** - partial payment received
- **Paid** - full payment received

## Order details

The detail page shows your line items, quantities, prices, taxes, discounts, and the total amount. You can refer back to this for payment and reconciliation.

## Common questions

**Q: How do I make a payment?**

Payment methods are handled outside PM One. Check with the event organizer for bank transfer details or other payment options.

**Q: My order status has not changed in a while.**

Contact the event organizer to check on your order's progress.
