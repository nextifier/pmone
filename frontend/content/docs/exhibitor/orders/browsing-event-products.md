The event organizer sets up a catalog of products and services you can order for your booth: electricity, furniture, signage, badges, and more. Browse the catalog before placing your order.

## What you will learn

- How to view the product catalog
- How to understand pricing and categories

## Viewing products

1. Navigate to your brand-event
2. Go to the **Orders** or **Order form** section
3. You will see the product catalog organized by category

Each product shows its name, description, unit price, and tax rate.

## Product categories

Products are grouped by category (e.g., "Electrical", "Furniture", "Signage"). Browse by category to find what you need.

## Pricing

Prices are listed before tax. The tax rate is shown per product and gets calculated when you add items to your order.

## Order period

The event organizer sets a window during which you can place orders. Check the order form page for deadline information. Late orders may incur penalty fees.

## Common questions

**Q: I do not see any products.**

The product catalog might not be set up yet for your event. Contact the event organizer.

**Q: Can I request a product that is not listed?**

Contact the event organizer directly for special requests. The catalog contains standard offerings.
