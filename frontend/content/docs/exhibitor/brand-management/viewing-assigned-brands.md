When the event organizer adds you to a brand, that brand appears in your account. You might manage one brand or several, depending on your organization.

## What you will learn

- How to see your assigned brands
- How to navigate between brands and events

## Viewing your brands

From the dashboard, you will see your brand-event cards. You can also:

1. Check the sidebar or navigation for a **My brands** or **Brands** link
2. Click it to see all brands linked to your account

Each brand shows its name, logo, and the events it participates in.

## Navigating to an event

Click a brand card, then select the event you want to work with. This takes you to the brand-event view where you can manage promotion posts, orders, and documents for that specific event.

## Common questions

**Q: I do not see any brands on my dashboard.**

The event organizer has not linked your account to a brand yet. Contact them to get assigned.

**Q: Can I be part of more than one brand?**

Yes. If your company operates multiple brands at different events, you can be added to each one.
