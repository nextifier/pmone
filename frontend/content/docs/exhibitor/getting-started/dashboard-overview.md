Your dashboard is the home screen after login. It shows your profile status, assigned brands, and a checklist of things to complete for each event.

## What you will learn

- How to read the dashboard
- What the progress checklist tracks

## Dashboard layout

### Profile status

The top section shows whether your personal profile is complete. You will see reminders if your name, phone number, job title, or company name is missing. Completing your profile helps the event organizer identify you.

### Your brand-events

Below the profile section, you will find cards for each event your brand is participating in. Each card shows:

- **Event name** and dates
- **Booth number** and type
- **Progress checklist** with completion indicators

### Progress checklist

For each brand-event, the dashboard tracks:

- **Event rules** - have you agreed to the event's terms and conditions?
- **Operational documents** - have you submitted all required files?
- **Promotion posts** - how many posts you have created vs. the limit
- **Orders** - number of orders submitted

Green checkmarks mean you are done. Open items need your attention.

## Navigation

From the dashboard, click any brand-event card to go straight to that event's section and start working on incomplete items.

## Common questions

**Q: I see multiple brand-events. What does that mean?**

Your account might be linked to more than one brand, or your brand participates in multiple events. Each card represents one brand at one event.

**Q: The dashboard says my profile is incomplete but I filled everything in.**

Check that all required fields are saved: name, phone number, job title, and company name. Click through to your profile settings to verify.
