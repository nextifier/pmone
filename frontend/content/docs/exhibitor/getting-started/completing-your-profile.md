The event organizer needs your contact details for badge printing, communication, and event coordination. Complete your profile early so nothing holds up the process.

## What you will learn

- Which fields to fill in
- How to upload your photo

## Editing your profile

1. Click your avatar in the top-right corner
2. Select **Profile** from the dropdown
3. Fill in: display name, phone number, job title, and company name
4. Upload a profile photo (optional but recommended for team identification)
5. Click **Save**

## Required fields

The dashboard marks your profile as incomplete if any of these are missing:

- Display name
- Phone number
- Job title
- Company name

Fill in all four and the profile status on your dashboard turns green.

## Common questions

**Q: Who can see my profile information?**

The event organizer (staff) can see your profile. Other exhibitors cannot see your personal details.

**Q: Can I update my profile later?**

Yes. You can change your details anytime from the profile settings page.
