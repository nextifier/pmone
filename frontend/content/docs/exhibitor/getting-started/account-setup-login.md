You received an email invitation from the event organizer with your login credentials. This guide walks you through your first login and account setup.

## What you will learn

- How to log in for the first time
- How to set or change your password
- How to switch between English and Chinese

## Your first login

1. Open the link from your invitation email
2. Enter your email address and the password provided
3. Click **Sign In**

If your invitation included a temporary password, you will want to change it after logging in (see below).

### Google sign-in

If you prefer, you can sign in with Google using the **Sign in with Google** button. Use the same email address that received the invitation.

## Changing your password

1. Click your avatar in the top-right corner
2. Go to **Settings > Password**
3. Enter your current password, then your new password
4. Click **Update password**

## Switching language

PM One supports English and Chinese for exhibitors. To switch:

1. Look for the language icon in the header bar
2. Click it and select your preferred language
3. The interface updates right away

## Common questions

**Q: I did not receive an invitation email.**

Check your spam folder. If it is not there, contact the event organizer and ask them to resend or share the login URL and credentials directly.

**Q: I forgot my password.**

Click **Forgot password** on the login page. Enter your email address and you will receive a reset link.

**Q: Can I create my own account?**

Exhibitor accounts are created by the event organizer. You cannot self-register as an exhibitor.
