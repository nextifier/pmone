Programs and rundown give your event a structured schedule. Programs define the tracks or session categories, while the rundown fills in the actual timeline.

## What you will learn

- How to create programs
- How to build out the rundown schedule

## Programs

Programs are the high-level tracks of your event (e.g., "Main Stage", "Workshop Room A", "Seminar Hall").

1. Open your event and go to **Content > Programs**
2. Click **Create** to add a new program
3. Give it a name and description
4. Click **Save**

## Rundown

The rundown is the detailed timeline: what happens, when, and where.

1. Go to **Content > Rundown**
2. Click **Create** to add a time slot
3. Fill in the session title, start/end time, description, and which program it belongs to
4. Click **Save**

### Reordering

Sessions sort by time automatically. You can also drag and drop to fine-tune the display order within the same time block.

## How it appears on the website

The event website displays the rundown as a timeline grouped by program. Visitors can filter by track to find sessions they care about.

## Common questions

**Q: Can I assign speakers to a session?**

Speaker management is handled through the **Guests and speakers** section. You can reference speakers in your session descriptions.
