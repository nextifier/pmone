Business categories for contacts work similarly to the ones for brands. They help you classify contacts by industry, role, or interest so you can filter and export targeted lists.

## What you will learn

- How to create categories
- How to assign categories to contacts
- How to import and export categories

## Creating categories

1. Go to **Contact List > Business categories**
2. Click **Create**
3. Enter the category name
4. Click **Save**

## Reordering

Drag and drop categories to change their display order in filters and dropdown menus.

## Assigning to contacts

When creating or editing a contact, select one or more business categories from the dropdown. These tags then become available as filters on the contact list.

## Import and export

Use **Import** to upload categories from an Excel file, or **Export** to download the current list. The template file shows the expected format.

## Common questions

**Q: Are these the same as project business categories?**

No. Contact business categories are separate from the ones used for brands within projects. They serve a similar purpose but are independent lists.

**Q: Can I delete a category that has contacts assigned to it?**

Yes. Deleting a category removes the tag from all contacts, but does not delete the contacts themselves.
